The Beginning

Founded in 2023, Zen Tea began with a simple mission: to bring the authentic taste of Taiwanese tea culture to the modern world.

We believe that milk tea isn't just a drink; it's a moment of pause in a busy day. We traveled across Asia to find the perfect tea farms, ensuring that every leaf brewed in our shop meets the highest standard of quality.
